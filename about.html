About Us

Who we are

introduction

❝Prime Consulting Office❞

It is a fast growing architectural firm, in the short time since its establishment PCO has been a part of a variety of key projects in the Egyptian commercial market. At PCO we pride ourselves with our ability to fully integrate our work with other disciplines through as well as finding integrated solution for the good of the project. We also work with both national and international codes.

PCO’s multi-disciplinary capabilities has allowed it to handle each project with great efficiency, taking on different roles and tasks varying from design and visualization to execution drawings and site management.

vision & Mission

vision

PCO Aims to produce high quality designs with efficient procedures to improve the Egyptian market in terms of project functionality and aesthetics

Mission

PCO strives to produce projects with an efficient workflow in each stage, while introducing new designs in order to improve the standard of projects to meet the needs of the users. PCO targets to satisfy the client’s vision in creating a high standard project to stand out in an ever-growing market, allowing it to become a landmark and defining point within its context.
